Synod Branding

Brand Definition: Messaging and Design

In 2012, this longtime residential services provider needed to better position themselves as the leading agency for not only the homeless, but also the mentally ill and other marginalized populations.


I designed this simple wordmark identity to signify the connectedness of the community. When one struggles, we all do. The visual identity system included color palette, a chain link graphic pattern, and other elements delivered in a brand style guide. Additionally, I provided photographic art direction for the staff features, which are displayed on the responsive website and highlighted in printed collateral materials.

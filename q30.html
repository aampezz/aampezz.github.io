Q's 30th Anniversary Animation

Storyboard, Art Direction, Illustration

In 2011, Q LTD celebrated its 30th anniversary, a significant achievement for a small, independent design studio. We knew that our success was due to all the wonderful people who had been a part of the Q family throughout the years, including employees, clients, and friends.


While we marked the occasion with a proper party, we also wanted to create a digital experience for those who couldn't join us in person. The website we created allows people to submit memories and other information, like decade or volleyball team member, and sort to find similar Qsters.


I led the design and production of a short promotional animation that served as an introduction to the site, telling the 30-year story in two minutes. We also used it in other press promotions.

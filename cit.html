Inspired Teaching Strategy

Customer Journey Mapping

Beginning in January 2013, I led an engagement with the Center for Inspired Teaching to determine how they could expand their Washington, DC-based teacher professional development (PD) program nationwide. In the end, we delivered not only that strategy for creating sustainable revenue flow, but also a marketing strategy to build national awareness about what Inspired Teaching is and how it improves educational practice and results, appealing to a broader community audience.


Our research process included brainstorming workshops with the Inspired Teaching team, observation of teachers and professional development sessions, as well as interviews and surveys from 25 teachers nationwide (1/3 customers and 2/3 non-customers).

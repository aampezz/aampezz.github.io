Kid's Eye View Mobile App

UX Research and Design

In 2013, a team of graduate students in Human Computer Interaction and Education and I designed this mobile application supporting Social Science inquiry with 2nd-grade students. The application encourages young citizens to understand and engage in their local community, an objective aligned with Michigan grade level content standards (GLCS), and bridges the gap between in-school lessons and off-site activities.


Our team created user personas, which included students, teachers, and parents, and detailed use cases. Our final design took the form of medium-fidelity wireframes.

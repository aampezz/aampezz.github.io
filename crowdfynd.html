Crowdfynd Mobile App

UX Design

In early 2014, I worked with Crowdfynd, a Chicago startup trying to help businesses increase efficiency and customer service offerings through a high tech lost and found platform.


Crowdfynd has an existing consumer-facing app, but they needed to redesign the entrance screens and streamline the photo uploading process. I led an affinity diagramming exercise to determine major pain points in the existing app, then used those insights to create wireframes and, ultimately, high fidelity mock-ups.
